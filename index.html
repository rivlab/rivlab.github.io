About Us

Welcome to the Robot Intelligence and Vision Laboratory (RIV Lab) at Bu-Ali Sina University.

Established in 2008 by Dr. Khotanlou, our lab has been a breeding ground for talented researchers specializing in various aspects of Artificial Intelligence, including Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, Fuzzy Systems, and Medical Image Processing.

With a dual focus on Robot Intelligence and Vision research, our team is advancing Human-Robot Interaction, Social Path-Planning, Human Gestures, 3D Face Recognition, Generative AI, and Multimodal Vision Systems. Our ultimate goal is to develop multimodal, efficient, and responsible AI systems that can better recognize, understand, and emulate our dynamic world.

How to Join Us

Thank you for your interest in joining our lab. We continually offer opportunities for BSc, MSc, and PhD theses aligned with our ongoing research. If you wish to join us, please follow these steps:

For PhD/MSc Students:

1.

Enroll in Bu-Ali Sina University and take at least one course with Dr. Khotanlou.

2.

Contact Dr. Khotanlou directly to express your interest in joining the lab.

For BSc Students:

1.

Enroll in Bu-Ali Sina University.

2.

Send your CV and academic transcript, highlighting your professional and academic achievements, to the following email address: riv@basu.ac.ir

All Applicants Should Have:

  • A strong interest in our research areas
  • Proficient programming skills, particularly in Python
  • Knowledge or experience in machine learning and deep learning

We look forward to welcoming passionate and skilled individuals to our team.
