About Us

Welcome to the Robot Intelligence and Vision Laboratory (RIV Lab) at Bu-Ali Sina University.

Established in 2008 by Dr. Khotanlou, our lab has been a breeding ground for talented researchers specializing in various aspects of Artificial Intelligence, including Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition, Fuzzy Systems, and Medical Image Processing.

With a dual focus on Robot Intelligence and Vision research, our team is advancing Human-Robot Interaction, Social Path-Planning, Human Gestures, 3D Face Recognition, Generative AI, and Multimodal Vision Systems. Our ultimate goal is to develop multimodal, efficient, and responsible AI systems that can better recognize, understand, and emulate our dynamic world.
